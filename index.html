How TrainLink Works

Three simple steps to transform your fitness routine

1

Create Your Workout Library

Add your favorite workouts to your personal library. Include YouTube videos, set durations, and categorize by difficulty. Choose from different workout types like HIIT, Cardio, Strength Training, Yoga and more.

2

Schedule Your Routine

Set up your workout schedule with flexible recurring options. Daily, weekly, or custom patterns help you stay consistent. View your entire fitness plan at a glance with the intuitive calendar interface.

Complete workouts, log your activity, and watch your progress grow over time. Review your workout history to stay motivated and see how far you've come. Your personal dashboard shows all your upcoming workouts and recent activity.
