Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

Introduction

Welcome to TrainLink. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

Data We Collect

When you use TrainLink, we collect and process the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, including pages visited, time spent on pages, and features used.
  • Workout Data: Information you voluntarily provide about your workouts, schedules, and logs.
  • Device Information: Information about the device you use to access our service, including browser type, IP address, and operating system.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: Information collected through cookies and similar technologies as described in our Cookie Policy section.

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To improve and personalize your experience
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize our website
  • To communicate with you about service-related matters
  • To display relevant advertising

Data Storage

TrainLink primarily stores your workout data in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Your workout data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not store your workout data on our servers
  • Your data remains on your device unless you clear your browser data

However, we do collect anonymous usage data through Google Analytics to help us improve our service.

Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (Google Analytics).
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements (Google AdSense).

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our service.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: To analyze usage of our website. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense: To display advertisements. Google Ads Policy

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • The right to erasure of your personal data
  • The right to restrict processing of your personal data
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object to processing of your personal data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
